Riyadh

Some of the famous places to visit in Riyadh are:

  • Kingdom Centre Tower: A modern skyscraper that offers panoramic views of the city
  • Diriyah Historical District: A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its mud-brick structures and historical significance.
  • Masmak Fortress: A historical fort that played a significant role in the kingdom's unification.
  • National Museum of Saudi Arabia: A museum that showcases the rich history and culture of Saudi Arabia.
  • Riyadh Zoo: A popular attraction with a variety of animals and recreational areas.
