Alula

Some of the famous places to visit in alula are:

  • Madain Saleh (Al-Hijr): Also known as Al-Hijr, Madain Saleh is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most important archaeological sites in Saudi Arabia.
  • AlUla Old Town: Explore the historic AlUla Old Town, which showcases traditional mud-brick buildings and narrow alleyways.
  • Elephant Rock: As its name suggests, Elephant Rock is a unique rock formation resembling an elephant.
  • AlUla Museum: The AlUla Museum offers insights into the history, archaeology, and heritage of the region.
