Makkah

Some of the famous places to visit in Makkah are:

  • Masjid al-Haram: The Grand Mosque, known as Masjid al-Haram, is the holiest site in Islam.
  • Zamzam Well: Situated near the Kaaba, the Zamzam Well is a source of blessed water.
  • Jabal al-Nour: Jabal al-Nour, meaning the "Mountain of Light," is a prominent mountain in Makkah.
  • Abraj Al-Bait Clock Tower: This iconic skyscraper complex stands adjacent to Masjid al-Haram.
